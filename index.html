Why SpendSmart

Everything a student needs

Built specifically for BTech students managing pocket money, hostel expenses, and monthly budgets.

🎯
Monthly Budget Alerts
Follow the 50/30/20 rule — allocate 50% to Needs, 30% to Wants, and 20% to Savings. Set your monthly budget and SpendSmart warns you before you overspend.
📊
Visual Analytics
See exactly where your money goes with a category doughnut chart and a 7-day bar chart. Spot overspending on Dining, Hobbies, or Subscriptions before it becomes a habit.
💡
Pay Yourself First
Move a fixed amount into savings the day you receive pocket money — before spending anything else. SpendSmart tracks what's left so you always stick to the plan.
🗂️
8 Smart Categories
Covers every student spending bucket — Food, Bills, Health, Fun, and more. Categorise every rupee and instantly see which habits are draining your wallet.
🛡️
Build an Iron Shield
Aim to save 3–6 months of basic living expenses as an emergency fund. Use SpendSmart's monthly overview to find where you can cut back and save more each month.
⏳
The 24-Hour Rule
Before logging a big purchase in Shopping or Fun, pause for 24 hours. If you still want it tomorrow, it's worth it. SpendSmart keeps you honest and impulse-proof.
